# Axiom 2 — Non-Orientable Vacuum Manifold

**Enunciation**
The vacuum manifold is the non-orientable, simply-connected quotient
$$\mathcal{M}_{vac} = (S^3 \times S^1) / \mathbb{Z}_2$$
with $\mathbb{Z}_2$ action $(g,\theta) \sim (-g, \theta+\pi)$.

**Topological invariants**
$w_1(\mathcal{M}_{vac}) \neq 0$,   $\pi_1(\mathcal{M}_{vac}) = 1$,   $\chi(\mathcal{M}_{vac}) = 0$.

**Zero-mode theorem**
Every non-trivial SU(2) gauge configuration on $\mathcal{M}_{vac}$ has exactly half the fermionic zero modes of the orientable double cover.

**Universal consequence**
In all instanton and sphaleron processes:
$$1/(16\pi^2) \rightarrow 1/(8\pi^2)$$
to all orders in perturbation theory (topologically protected).
